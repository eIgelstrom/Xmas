Augmented Reality Crappy Christmas

Do you like Christmas as much as The Grinch does? Then this is the perfect Christmas game for you! Here you have the chance to ruin Christmas and have fun while doing it.

How to Play

Aim your camera on your phone towards the 3D printed Christmas stand. Then you will see the Christmas tree pop up from the stand in AR. You will also see a lot of gifts. Open a present by tapping on it. Then you can pick it up and throw it on the Christmas tree. If you hit the tree it will catch fire. Throw as many gifts in the tree as you can! On the left you see a bar that tells you how much CO2 was required to create that gift.
